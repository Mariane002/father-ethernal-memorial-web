Memorialization Options

At Our Father Eternal and Memorial Garden, we offer a range of memorialization options to honor your loved ones. Choose the plan that best fits your needs and desires.

  • Traditional Burial Plots
  • Cremation Gardens
  • Mausoleum
  • Memorial Walls
  • Custom Memorial Options

Traditional Burial Plots

Peaceful burial plots surrounded by beautiful gardens and trees.

Our traditional burial plots provide a serene resting place surrounded by the natural beauty of our gardens. Each plot offers a peaceful environment for families to visit and pay their respects.

Cremation Gardens

Beautiful settings for the cremated remains of your loved ones, including cremation benches, trees, and urns.

Our cremation gardens offer various memorial options such as cremation benches, cremation trees, and cremation urns. Each garden is designed to provide a tranquil setting for the cremated remains of your loved ones.

Mausoleum

Our stately mausoleum provides an elegant and dignified resting place, offering both individual and family crypts.

Our stately mausoleum offers an elegant and dignified resting place. With options for both individual and family crypts, the mausoleum provides a serene and secure environment for honoring your loved ones.

Memorial Walls

Unique and durable walls for personalized and fitting tributes.

Our memorial walls are crafted from beautiful and durable materials, offering a unique way to honor your loved ones. Choose from a variety of design options to create a personalized tribute.

Custom Memorial Options

Create a truly unique tribute that reflects the life and legacy of your loved one.

For those seeking a truly unique and personalized tribute, we offer custom memorial options. Our team will work with you to explore all possibilities and create a memorial that reflects the life and legacy of your loved one.
